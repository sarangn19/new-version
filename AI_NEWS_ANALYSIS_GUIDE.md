# AI News Analysis Guide

## Overview

The AI News Analysis feature helps UPSC aspirants understand the exam relevance of current affairs and stay updated with the most important news for their preparation.

## How It Works

### Automatic News Processing
- **Real-time Updates**: News articles are fetched from verified sources every hour
- **AI Summarization**: Each article is automatically summarized using advanced AI
- **UPSC Relevance Scoring**: Articles are rated for their importance to UPSC preparation
- **Subject Categorization**: News is automatically sorted by UPSC subjects

### Supported News Sources
- **Government Sources**: PIB, Press releases, Official statements
- **Major Publications**: The Hindu, Indian Express, Times of India
- **Specialized Sources**: Economic Times, Business Standard for economics news
- **International Sources**: BBC, Reuters for international affairs

## Using News Analysis

### Accessing News Analysis

1. **News Page**: Navigate to the News section from the sidebar
2. **AI Assistant**: Switch to News Analysis mode in the chat
3. **Quick Analysis**: Use the floating AI button on any page

### Getting Analysis

**Method 1: Browse Analyzed News**
1. Go to the News page
2. View AI-summarized articles with relevance scores
3. Click on any article for detailed analysis

**Method 2: Ask the AI Assistant**
1. Switch to News Analysis mode
2. Paste a news article or URL
3. Ask specific questions about current events

**Method 3: Daily Briefings**
1. Ask "What are today's important news for UPSC?"
2. Request subject-specific updates: "Economics news this week"
3. Get exam-focused summaries: "Mains-relevant current affairs"

## Advanced Features

### Custom News Alerts

**Setting Up Alerts:**
1. Go to News Analysis settings
2. Select subjects of interest
3. Set relevance threshold (High/Medium/Low)
4. Choose notification frequency

**Alert Types:**
- **Breaking News**: Immediate notifications for high-relevance news
- **Daily Digest**: Summary of important news each evening
- **Weekly Roundup**: Comprehensive weekly analysis
- **Subject-Specific**: Alerts for specific subjects only

### News Bookmarking

**Save Important News:**
- Click the bookmark icon on any analyzed article
- Add personal notes and tags
- Create custom categories
- Export bookmarked news as study material

**Organization Features:**
- Filter by subject, date, or relevance
- Search through saved articles
- Create themed collections (e.g., "Budget 2024", "Climate Summit")
- Share collections with study groups

### Integration with Study Materials

**Convert News to Study Content:**
- **Notes**: Save detailed analysis as study notes
- **Flashcards**: Create fact-based flashcards from news
- **MCQs**: Generate practice questions from current affairs
- **Essays**: Use news as examples in essay practice

## Best Practices

### Daily News Routine

**Morning (10 minutes):**
1. Check overnight high-relevance news
2. Read AI summaries of top 5 articles
3. Bookmark important items for detailed study

**Evening (20 minutes):**
1. Review the day's complete news digest
2. Read detailed analysis of bookmarked articles
3. Connect news items to static syllabus topics
4. Update current affairs notes

### Weekly Review

**Sunday Planning:**
1. Review the week's most important news
2. Identify trending topics and themes
3. Create connections between different news items
4. Plan focused study for emerging topics

### Monthly Consolidation

**Month-End Activities:**
1. Export all high-relevance news as study material
2. Create subject-wise current affairs compilations
3. Identify gaps in coverage and fill them
4. Practice MCQs based on the month's news

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**News Not Loading:**
- Check internet connection
- Refresh the page
- Clear browser cache

**Analysis Not Detailed Enough:**
- Ask specific follow-up questions
- Request analysis from different angles
- Use the "Explain in detail" option

**Relevance Scoring Seems Wrong:**
- Remember scoring is based on general UPSC relevance
- Your optional subject may make some news more relevant for you
- Use the feedback option to improve AI accuracy

### Getting Better Results

**Improve Analysis Quality:**
- Provide context when asking questions
- Specify your preparation stage (beginner/advanced)
- Mention your optional subject for personalized relevance

**Optimize for Your Needs:**
- Customize relevance thresholds in settings
- Set subject preferences based on your weak areas
- Use tags and categories that match your study system
