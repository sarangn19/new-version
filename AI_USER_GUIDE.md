## Getting Started

### Accessing the AI Assistant

1. **Main AI Chat**: Click on the "AI Assistant" button in the sidebar or navigate to the chatbot page
2. **Quick Access**: Use the floating AI button (blue circle with "AI") on study pages
3. **Keyboard Shortcut**: Press `Ctrl + Shift + A` to quickly open the AI assistant

### First Time Setup

The AI assistant works immediately without any setup required. However, you can customize your experience:

1. **Profile Integration**: Your AI responses are personalized based on your profile preferences
2. **Study Preferences**: The AI adapts to your study schedule and goals from your profile settings
3. **Theme Compatibility**: The AI interface automatically matches your chosen theme

## Chat Interface

### Sending Messages

1. **Text Input**: Type your question in the input box
2. **Send Options**:

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**AI Not Responding**:
- Check your internet connection
- Refresh the page and try again
- Clear browser cache if issues persist

**Slow Response Times**:
- AI responses may take 2-5 seconds
- Complex queries take longer to process
- Multiple users may cause temporary delays

**Saving Issues**:
- Ensure you're logged in to your account
- Check if you have sufficient storage space
- Try refreshing the page and saving again

**Mode Switching Problems**:
- Wait for current response to complete
- Clear conversation and try switching modes
- Refresh the page if mode doesn't change

### Getting Help

**In-App Support**:
- Use the help button in the AI chat interface
- Check the FAQ section in your profile
- Contact support through the feedback form

**Community Support**:
- Visit the Community page for user discussions
- Share tips and tricks with other users
- Report bugs and suggest improvements

### Performance Optimization

**For Better Experience**:
- Use latest version of Chrome, Firefox, or Safari
- Ensure stable internet connection (minimum 1 Mbps)
- Close unnecessary browser tabs
- Clear browser cache weekly

**Storage Management**:
- Regularly export important responses
- Delete old conversation history if needed
- Organize saved content with proper tags
